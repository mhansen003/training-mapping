üí° About DocuTech Integration

DocuTech (First American Docutech ConformX) is CMG's loan document vendor integrated with Byte LOS. Documents are generated in DocuTech and "pushed" to Byte, where borrowers can e-sign. Signed documents "push back" to DocuTech automatically. For technical support, contact DocuTechSupport@cmgfi.com.
