Overview

Understanding when and why to enter credentials

Integration Purpose

Byte and Clear LOS systems collaborate to offer automated originating tools for key vendor services, eliminating the need for manual sign-ins on loans by programming credit credentials into Byte.

Services Requiring Credentials

3
Credit, AUS, Flood

Credit Vendors

3
Xactus, Birchwood, Partners

AUS Systems

2
DU (Fannie) + LP (Freddie)

When to Enter Credentials

Scenario 1: New CMG Employee
Initial credentials issued upon licensure or role approval. Follow Onboarding email to set password and IP address, then proceed to Byte.
Scenario 2: Password Change Required
Credit vendor prompts periodic password change, or security requires it. Follow vendor instructions first, then update Byte.

Configure Both Sections

Critical: Both Sections Required

Defaults and the credit agency must be set correctly for successful AUS runs. You must configure both the Standard section AND the Early Assessment section.

DU - Credit Agencies - Standard

  • + Complete with your full-report vendor credentials
  • + Options: Xactus, Birchwood, or Partners Credit
  • + Enter User Name and Password
  • + Initial Credit Action: Reissue Report

DU - Early Assessment (Soft Pull)

  • ! Always use Xactus credentials
  • + All Soft Pulls use Xactus regardless of primary vendor
  • + Complete with Xactus-issued credentials
  • + Initial Credit Action: Reissue Credit

Final Step

Click Close in the bottom right of the screen. Then proceed with the AUS Order task in the appropriate LOS of Clear or Byte for any loan.
