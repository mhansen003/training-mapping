## Quick Reference by Screen

### Home Screen
- Agency Case No
- Parcel No
- Property Type
- Loan Info (PITI, Other Financing)
- Registration and Lock Info (Lock Exp. Date)

### Overview Panel
- Loan Amount, Interest Rate, LTV, DTI Ratios
- Loan Type, Occupancy, Prop Type
- Lock Expiration
- Cash from/to Borrower
- Earliest Closing Date (via Details hyperlink)

### URLA Borrower Info
- Personal Information (Contact, AKAs)
- Loan and Property Information
- Assets, Liabilities, Declarations
- Employment and Income
- Loan Identifiers

### URLA Lender Loan Info
- Mortgage Loan Information
- Details of Transaction
- Proposed Monthly Payment
- Title Information
- Loan Identifiers

### Loan Estimate Pages
- **Page 1**: Interest Rate, Parcel No, Disclosure History
- **Page 2**: Critical Dates, Details of Transaction, Escrow, Appraisal Info

### Closing Screens
- Title (Endorsements, Parcel No, Vesting)
- Flood, AKAs, General, Sellers

### Doc Prep
- Title Company, Flood Cert
- Builder/Seller, Agent Information
- CPL Expiration, CoC Requested

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Use Overview Panel First** - Many key loan metrics are visible at a glance
2. **Check Multiple Locations** - Some fields sync across screens, verify consistency
3. **Processing Log for Dates** - Appraisal ordered/received dates are also logged here
4. **Status Screen for Expirations** - Rate lock and underwriting expiration dates in one place
5. **Application Summary** - Quick snapshot of ratios, reserves, and transaction details
