Why Store Your Signature?

Your signature stored in Byte allows for electronic document signing within the Loan Origination System. This is a one-time setup process that enables you to digitally sign loan documents without printing, signing, and scanning each individual document.

CRITICAL: BLACK Ink Only

You MUST use BLACK ink for your signature. Blue ink, pencil, or other colors will NOT be accepted and your form will be returned. This is required for optimal digital capture and document processing.

Signature Placement Guidelines

Where to Sign?
Sign in the open space provided on the form - this is the large blank area in the middle of the page
Keep your signature centered in the available space
Sign naturally - use your normal signature as it appears on official documents
Do NOT sign on the "Name:" line at the bottom - that's for printing your name

Signature Quality Tips

Sign on a flat, hard surface for consistent line weight. Avoid signing on soft surfaces like a stack of papers, as this can cause uneven ink distribution and affect scan quality.

# Signature to Store in Byte

## Overview
This process allows you to register your signature in the Byte Loan Origination System for electronic document signing.

## Prerequisites
- Printer access
- BLACK ink pen (required)
- Scanner access
- Email access

## Steps

### Step 1: Print the Form
1. Open the Signature Form PDF
2. Print on standard letter paper (8.5" x 11")
3. Use 100% scale - do not shrink or enlarge

### Step 2: Sign Your Name
1. Locate the open space in the middle of the form
2. Sign using BLACK INK ONLY
3. Use your normal signature as it appears on official documents
4. Do NOT sign on the "Name:" line

### Step 3: Print Your Name
1. Locate the "Name:" line at the bottom of the form
2. PRINT (do not sign) your full legal name
3. Write legibly in block letters

### Step 4: Scan and Submit
1. Scan the completed form as a PDF
2. Use 200-300 DPI resolution
3. Email to: bytesupport@cmgfi.com
4. Processing time: 1-2 business days

## Important Notes
- BLACK ink is REQUIRED - blue or other colors will be rejected
- Print your name, don't sign it on the name line
- Keep file size under 5 MB

## Support Contact
Email: bytesupport@cmgfi.com
