When Manual Byte Updates Are Required

Scenario 1: Wet Signature
Borrower receives disclosures electronically but prints and wet signs instead of e-signing
Branch employee emails Closing/Processing/Disclosure Manager
Scenario 2: Mailbox Rule
Mailbox rule applies and Byte needs manual update to show borrower receipt
Policy & Procedure assists in evaluating wait period override

Policy Reminder

It is against Company policy to email any disclosures to the borrower's email without proper consent.

Who Performs Updates

Only Managers (Closing, Processing, or Disclosure) are authorized to update Byte Logs. Branch employees must notify the appropriate manager.

# Updating Byte Log Entries

## Overview
When borrowers choose to wet-sign (print and sign) disclosures instead of e-signing, managers must manually update Byte to reflect disclosure receipt for accurate Earliest Allowable Closing Date calculation.

## When Manual Updates Are Required
1. Borrower prints and wet-signs disclosures instead of e-signing
2. Mailbox rule applies and system needs manual confirmation

## Verification Process

### DocuTech (ConformX)
1. Log into: https://www.conformx.com/Login.aspx
2. Click Solex > Select Vault
3. Search by date range and loan number
4. Click 3-dot menu > Audit Log
5. Verify "Login Success" in Signer Evidence Trail

### IDS (Fulfillment Dashboard)
1. Log into: https://fulfillment.idsdoc.com/Account/Login
2. Search by Loan Number
3. Expand borrower (+)
4. Verify "Documents Electronically Received" has timestamp

## Byte Update Process
1. Open file > Overview > Details > Disclosure History
2. Double-click timeline entry needing update
3. Click Document Package tab
4. Double-click borrower in Signers section
5. Enter Hard-Copy Mailed: MM/DD/YEAR 8:00 AM
6. Enter Hard-Copy Received: MM/DD/YEAR
7. Click Close, repeat for all borrowers
8. Update Delivery Method on General tab
9. Click Ok to save

## Final Steps
- Verify Earliest Closing Date updated correctly
- Add Conversation Log entry documenting the override

## Important Notes
- Only Managers can perform these updates
- Never email disclosures without proper consent
- Always verify with vendor audit logs before updating
