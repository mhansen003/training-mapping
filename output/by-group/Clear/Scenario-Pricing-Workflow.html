Conventional MI: BestX vs Manual

ü§ñ BestX MI (Automated)

  • ‚úì Compares ALL PMI providers automatically
  • ‚úì Shows best rates from Arch, Enact, Essent, MGIC, National, Radian
  • ‚úì Click "Run MI" to execute
  • ‚úì First run excludes Radian (click Refresh)
  • ‚úó Cannot use with Scenario Optimizer

‚úèÔ∏è Manual MI

  • ‚úì Enter known MI factor directly
  • ‚úì Choose PMI Type: Monthly, Split, Upfront
  • ‚úì Enter % (e.g., 0.32%)
  • ‚úì Works with Scenario Optimizer
  • ‚úó Must know the factor in advance

‚ö†Ô∏è ARM Products Exception

For ARM options, you must click "Run MI" individually for each ARM product. They do not run simultaneously like Fixed Rate options.
