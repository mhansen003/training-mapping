Credit ordering fundamentals

üìä Credit Report Types

  • Soft Pull: No impact on credit score, used for prequalification scenarios
  • Hard Pull: Full tri-merge report, required for formal loan application
  • Joint vs Individual: Joint for shared applications, Individual for privacy-preferred borrowers

üí° Report Selection Tips

For joint applications with shared financials, select both borrowers for a joint report. The system will submit the request and return results within seconds.

üì• Import Process

  • Review Credit Report: Check the score and liabilities displayed in the Credit section.
  • Click Import Liabilities: Automatically populate the URLA liabilities section with credit report data.
  • Review & Match REO: For mortgage accounts, link them to the appropriate Real Estate Owned entries.
  • Verify Accuracy: Confirm all liability information is accurate and make corrections as needed.

üí° Best Practice

Always compare imported liabilities against borrower-provided documentation for accuracy. Discrepancies may indicate undisclosed debts or outdated credit information.
