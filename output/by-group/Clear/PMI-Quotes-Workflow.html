üõ°Ô∏è What is PMI?

Private Mortgage Insurance protects the lender if a borrower defaults on a conventional loan with less than 20% down payment. PMI is required when LTV exceeds 80% on conventional loans.

PMI Type Description Best For
Monthly Premium Added to monthly payment, cancellable Most borrowers, flexible
Single Premium One-time upfront payment Seller credits available
Split Premium Partial upfront + lower monthly Lowering DTI needed
Lender-Paid (LPMI) Built into rate, not cancellable Tax deduction benefit

‚ö†Ô∏è PMI Cancellation Rules

  • Borrower can request cancellation at 80% LTV
  • Good payment history required (no 30-day lates)
  • New appraisal may be required for early cancellation
