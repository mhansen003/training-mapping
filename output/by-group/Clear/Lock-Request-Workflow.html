üìã What is a Rate Lock?

A rate lock guarantees a specific interest rate and pricing for a defined period. Once locked, the rate is protected from market fluctuations until the lock expires or the loan closes.

Lock Component Description Typical Range
Lock Period Duration the rate is guaranteed 15, 30, 45, 60, 90 days
Lock Fee Cost for extended lock periods 0.125% - 0.375% per 15 days
Float Down Option to reduce rate if market improves 0.125% - 0.25% cost

üìß Lock Confirmation Notification

Once the Lock Desk approves your request, you will receive:

  • Email confirmation with lock details
  • Lock confirmation number
  • Rate sheet with final pricing
  • Lock expiration date and time

‚ö†Ô∏è Common Lock Rejection Reasons

  • Missing or expired AUS approval
  • Credit report older than 120 days
  • Incomplete property information
  • Loan amount exceeds program limits
  • DTI outside guideline parameters
