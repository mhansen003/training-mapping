ğŸ“‹ What is the Borrower Needs List?

The Borrower Needs List is a centralized document tracking system in CLEAR that manages all outstanding documents and conditions required from the borrower. It integrates with the borrower portal for easy document submission.

Condition Type Description Typical Items
PTD (Prior to Docs) Required before loan docs can be drawn Paystubs, bank statements, tax returns
PTF (Prior to Funding) Required before loan can fund Final VOE, insurance binder
PTC (Prior to Closing) Required before closing can occur Gift letter, updated credit
