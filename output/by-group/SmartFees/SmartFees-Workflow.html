üí∞ What is SmartFees?

SmartFees is CMG Financial's centralized fee provider management system integrated with Clear LOS. It enables:

  • Pre-approved provider database by state and county
  • Automated fee population on Loan Estimates
  • Real-time fee quote retrieval from providers
  • TRID tolerance tracking and alerts
  • Streamlined process to add new providers

‚ö†Ô∏è Why Accurate Fees Matter

  • TRID Compliance: Meeting tolerance requirements
  • Borrower Trust: No surprise costs at closing
  • Operational Efficiency: Pre-configured providers save time
  • Audit Protection: Documentation of fee sources

TRID Tolerance & Compliance

Understanding fee tolerance categories
Tolerance Fee Types Rule
Zero Lender origination charges, Transfer taxes, Fees to unaffiliated providers (cannot shop) Cannot increase at all
10% Cumulative Recording fees, Services borrower can shop for (if lender's provider used) Total cannot increase >10%
No Limit Prepaid interest, Property insurance, Services borrower shops for (using own provider) Can change without limit

Tolerance Indicators in Clear

üü¢ Green: Within Tolerance
üü° Yellow: Approaching Limit
üî¥ Red: Exceeds - Cure Required

‚ö†Ô∏è Tolerance Cure Process

If fees exceed tolerance at closing:

  1. Calculate overage amount
  2. CMG issues lender credit to borrower
  3. Document cure in loan file with explanation

üí° Prevention is Key

Using accurate SmartFees providers and keeping fee schedules current prevents most tolerance violations.
