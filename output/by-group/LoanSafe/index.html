System Overview

What is LoanSafe?

LoanSafe is CoreLogic's fraud detection and mitigation solution that analyzes loan data to identify mortgage fraud risk. It provides fraud risk scores, alerts, and recommendations to minimize potential fraud throughout the loan lifecycle.

Key Databases Searched

  • ✓ SSN Validation
  • ✓ MERS (Mortgage Registry)
  • ✓ NMLS/ASC (Licensing)
  • ✓ OFAC Sanctions List
  • ✓ SAM.gov Exclusions
  • ✓ HUD/LDP & FHFA SCP
  • ✓ Freddie Mac Exclusionary

When is LoanSafe Run?

  • 1 Initial: File Received Status
  • 2 Prior to UW Submission
  • 3 Final: CTC Requested

Report auto-runs when status changes to "File Received" (Submitted to Processing)

Role Responsibilities

  • Processor - Clear LOW alerts
  • Underwriter - Clear MED/HIGH alerts
  • Underwriter - Fraud Scores > 900
  • All - Add required parties

Alert Severity Guide

LOW Alerts

Cleared by: Processor/Account Manager

Minor discrepancies or informational flags that require acknowledgment but typically don't indicate fraud risk.

MEDIUM Alerts

Cleared by: Underwriter

Moderate risk indicators requiring documentation review and potential additional verification.

HIGH Alerts

Cleared by: Underwriter

Significant fraud indicators requiring thorough investigation, documentation, and potential escalation.

Fraud Score > 900

Action: Risk Fraud Manager Policy

High-risk loans requiring comprehensive review per Risk Fraud Manager Policy and potential escalation to management.
