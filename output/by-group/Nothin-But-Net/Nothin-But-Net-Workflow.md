## Overview

Nothin' But Net‚Ñ¢ is a process that allows for the borrower's current escrow balance to be netted from the payoff on a refinance. To be eligible, the borrower's existing mortgage must be currently serviced by CMG Financial.

**Benefits:** Less cash needed at closing for the borrower!

## Receive the Payoff

### Process

1. **CMG Servicing Concierge Team** reviews the request
2. Team reaches out to Requestor Email if additional info needed
3. Team orders netted payoff from Cenlar
4. Payoff is uploaded to Byte file
5. Copy emailed to Requestor Email address

**Turnaround:** Please allow 2-5 business days for payoff receipt.

## Important Information

### If Loan Doesn't Close or Rescinds

üö® **CRITICAL:** Notify `servicinghelp@cmgfi.com` **immediately** to avoid potential escrow issues on the existing loan.

### Low Escrow Balance Scenarios

CMG Servicing Concierge Team will reach out if:
- There is an **escrow shortage**
- There are **upcoming disbursements**
- Current escrow balance is **under $500**

### FHA to FHA Refinances

These do NOT need to follow the Nothin' But Net process. Request netted escrow payoffs **directly from Cenlar**.
