What is Velocify?

Velocify is CMG's lead management and distribution platform that powers the sales workflow. Key capabilities include:

  • Intelligent Lead Distribution: Routes leads to the right LO based on rules
  • Lead Prioritization: Surfaces the hottest leads for immediate action
  • Automated Actions: Triggers emails, SMS, and tasks automatically
  • Power Dialer: Click-to-call with disposition tracking
  • Performance Analytics: Real-time metrics on contact and conversion rates

Speed to Lead is Everything

Studies show contacting a lead within 5 minutes increases conversion by 400%. Velocify helps you achieve this with instant distribution and prioritized work queues.

Distribution Methods

Method How It Works Best For
Round Robin Leads distributed evenly across LOs in rotation Equal distribution, generic leads
Priority Routing Routes to highest-performing LOs first based on metrics High-value leads, conversion focus
Geographic Matches lead location to LO licensed states/territory Multi-state operations
Weighted Distributes based on assigned capacity percentages Mixed experience levels
Pull Model Leads go to queue; LOs claim from pool Competitive teams, shared pools

Rule Priority Order

Distribution rules are evaluated in order. The first matching rule wins:

  1. Specific source/campaign rules (highest priority)
  2. Geographic/state licensing rules
  3. Product type rules (VA, FHA, Jumbo, etc.)
  4. Default catchall rule (lowest priority)

When Distribution Fails

Leads fall to an unassigned queue when:

  • No LO licensed in lead's state
  • All LOs at capacity (lead limit reached)
  • No matching distribution rule exists
  • System cannot determine lead location

Action: Managers should monitor unassigned queue and manually route.

Trigger Timeframe Action

Queue Management Tips

  • Always work HOT leads first - they have the highest conversion
  • Use the priority view to see your most urgent leads at the top
  • Set status accurately to avoid unnecessary aging alerts
  • Check your queue every 15 minutes during peak hours

SMS Compliance Requirements

  • Lead must have opted in to receive SMS (consent required)
  • Include opt-out instructions in every message
  • Do not send before 8 AM or after 9 PM local time
  • Maximum 160 characters recommended for deliverability

Dial-IQ Features

  • Click-to-Call: One-click dialing from lead record
  • Local Presence: Display local area code to increase answer rates
  • Call Recording: Automatic recording for quality and compliance
  • Power Dialer: Auto-dial next lead when call ends
  • Call Logging: Automatic duration and outcome tracking

Call Disposition Codes

Disposition Meaning Next Action
Connected Spoke with lead/borrower Update status based on conversation
Voicemail Left message Schedule follow-up in 4-24 hours
No Answer No answer, no voicemail Retry in 2-4 hours
Wrong Number Number incorrect/disconnected Research alternate contact
DNC Request Do not call requested Add to DNC list immediately
Callback Scheduled Specific callback time set Create scheduled task

Dialer Best Practices

  • Always log a disposition after every call attempt
  • Use Power Dialer mode for high-volume calling sessions
  • Schedule callbacks for specific times when lead requests
  • Review call recordings to improve your pitch

The 5-Minute Rule

Research shows that contacting a lead within 5 minutes of submission increases your chances of connecting by 900% compared to waiting 30 minutes. Make your first attempt within 5 minutes - always.

Optimal Contact Cadence

Attempt Timing Method
1 0-5 minutes Call + SMS
2 15-30 minutes Call
3 1 hour Call + Email
4 4 hours Call
5 Next morning Call + SMS
6-8 Days 2-3 Call daily
9+ Days 4-14 Call every other day + nurture emails

Daily Workflow Recommendations

Time Block Activity Focus
8:00 - 8:30 AM Queue Review Prioritize HOT leads, plan callbacks
8:30 - 11:30 AM Power Dial Session Work through prioritized queue
11:30 - 12:30 PM Admin/Follow-up Update statuses, send follow-up emails
12:30 - 2:00 PM Power Dial Session Afternoon calling block
2:00 - 4:00 PM Scheduled Callbacks Honor callback appointments
4:00 - 5:00 PM Evening Prep Prepare for next day, final calls

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Waiting too long: Even 10 minutes reduces conversion significantly
  • Giving up too soon: 50% of sales happen after the 5th contact
  • Poor disposition logging: Inaccurate data hurts your metrics
  • Skipping voicemails: Voicemails trigger callbacks
  • Ignoring SMS: Text messages have 98% open rates
