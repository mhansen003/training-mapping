‚ùì When to Cancel

Cancel a Truv order when you need to stop borrower notifications and invitations. Common reasons include:

  • Borrower requests to stop text/email notifications
  • Order was placed in error
  • Loan was withdrawn or cancelled
  • Using alternative verification method instead
  • Wrong borrower or employer was selected
‚ö†Ô∏è Important

Orders can ONLY be cancelled through the Truv SSO website. They cannot be cancelled directly within CLEAR or Byte.

1

Open Single Sign-On (SSO) dashboard. Find Truv in Organizational Apps and click to open.

2

In the Truv left navigation menu, click Activity ‚Üí Orders.

3

Search for the Order

Use the search bar to find the order by borrower name, email, phone, employer, order ID, or loan number.

4

Open Actions Menu

Click the 3 ellipses (‚ãÆ) on the right end of the order row to open the Actions menu.

5

Select Cancel order from the Actions dropdown menu.

6

Confirm Cancellation

A confirmation dialog appears: "Are you sure you want to cancel this order? User will not be able to open the link." Click Cancel order button to confirm.
