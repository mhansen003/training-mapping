Employment & Income Documentation

Income verification and employment documentation
Pay Stubs - Reflect borrower(s) name, company info and YTD income; note any odd deductions
Income within HomeReady/Home Possible limits?
Written VOEs for all borrowers
W-2 Forms - Most recent 2 years, all employers, dates worked and position titles
Alimony/child support: Evidence payments received for last 3 months - Gross up per tax bracket
Most recent 2 years tax return if rental property on REO
IRS Transcripts from Credit Plus - Check findings for # years (2 yrs NHH; 3 yrs MCC non-targeted)

Self-Employed Documentation

Tax Returns - Personal for Sch C, 1120s & K-1s for S-Corp, 1120 for Corporation, 1065 for Partnership - Most recent 2 years
Proof of business existence from 3rd Party - Google search, state business search (must be in good standing)
