Best Practice: Email Communication

The preferred method of communication is via email. Include the following in your subject line:

  • Borrower's last name and complete loan number with prefix (RWE, RNW, RSR)
  • Nature of the request (Lock Extension, Program Change)
  • Loan program or product type (Conventional, Jumbo, 101, Non-QM, etc.)

The body of the email should be clear and concise. If something looks off, provide screenshots and examples.

General FAQs

Frequently asked questions about lock desk operations
How do I extend my lock? How much does it cost?
Lock extensions for most loans can be completed via Optimal Blue, using the Change Request icon, and selecting the Lock Extension radio button. The cost for Agency and Jumbo lock extension is 0.02 per day. For full instructions, please see the Optimal Blue Pricing Manual.
Does CMG offer overnight price protection?
Yes. Users should notify the lock desk via email prior to 8:30AM EST to hold pricing for the previous day. The user should then lock the loan in the system and contact the lock desk via email to adjust the pricing to the previous day. Please see the Retail Lock Policy for full detail.
How do I change loan parameters on my locked loan?
Changes to locked loans can be processed through Optimal Blue's Profile Change Request feature. Select the Change Request icon, choose the 'Profile Change' radio button, make the necessary changes to your loan amount, appraised value, etc., and select the lock icon to proceed with the changes.
I need to switch my loan from Fannie to Freddie or Conv to FHA. How do I do it?
Changes can be processed through Optimal Blue's Profile Change Request feature. Choose the Profile Change button, review the loan parameters, select the appropriate Loan Type, hit Submit, and you should see the list of eligible products and pricing. Hit the lock icon and proceed with the change.
My lock expired. How do I relock?
Relocks can be performed through the system by accessing OB through Byte/Clear for Agency and Jumbo products. Select the Change Request icon and then the radio button to Relock. For full instructions, please see the Optimal Blue Pricing Manual.
I need to change my rate or view rate options from when I initially locked. Where do I go?
Launch out to OB through Byte/Clear and go to the Change Request icon, select Profile Change, and hit submit. The rate stack from the original day will appear. If you wish to change the rate, select the new rate desired and hit the lock icon to proceed. If not, just close out of the page when done.
Rates have improved since I locked. Can I float down?
Yes. CMG offers a one-time float down option on Agency loans. Please refer to the Lock Policy (page #5) for additional information. Float down requests can be completed through the system by accessing OB through Byte/Clear. Select the Change Request icon and then the radio button to Float Down.
What is the difference between a General Lender Credit and a Net Lender Credit?

1. Net Lender Credit - A price improvement adjustment typically used to lower the discount points being charged to the borrower. A net lender credit resulting in an overage (price above par) can also be used toward the borrower's closing costs.

2. General Lender Credit - A lump sum lender credit that is itemized on the LE and CD. Once disclosed to the borrower, a general lender credit cannot be reduced or removed.

I cannot find this product in Optimal Blue. How do I price it?
Some products are handled manually as they are not automated in the system. Please reach out to the appropriate lock desk inbox:
  • AIO's - RetailLockDesk@cmgfi.com
  • Bond/HFA products - BondLocks@cmgfi.com
  • Chenoa - BondLocks@cmgfi.com
  • CMG 2nds - NonAgencyLocks@cmgfi.com
  • Near Miss and Alt A - NonAgencyLocks@cmgfi.com
I would like to lock my AIO loan. How do I lock it?
AIO products must be in UW Approved status prior to locking. Once it's approved, email RetailLockDesk@cmgfi.com with the loan number, last name of borrower, and the desired margin and we will lock it in for you.

Bonds and HFA Products Helpful Tips

Special procedures for Bond and Housing Finance Agency products
I have a bond lock. How do I get it locked in the system?
If you have the commitment completed from the HFA site, email BondLocks@cmgfi.com with the commitment confirmation along with the loan #. The desk will lock the loan in the LOS system. See The Bond/DPA Loan workflow and input guide for full detail.
I need to extend my bond lock.
Lock desk (BondLocks@cmgfi.com) requires an updated lock commitment from the HFA showing that the lock has been extended. (i.e., Lock commitment with new expiration date, email from bond agency, or extension form approved by the agency.) See The Bond/DPA Loan workflow and input guide for full detail.
Why do I not get the same lock expiration as the one on my commitment?
CMG adds time for delivery and settlement to each lock.

Items That Delay Bond Locking Process

  • When locking with a 1st, 2nd and 3rd loan, make sure the 2nd and 3rd are created in Byte and already synchronized
  • When making reservations on the HFA site, confirm it is for the total loan amount, not the base loan amount (account for the MIP). Provide base and total loan amount to prevent back and forth
  • Accuracy on property information: street address, city, and zip code
  • When requesting lock, attach a PDF of the actual commitment confirmation from the HFA site (not just a screenshot of the reservation page). There should be an actual confirmation so the Bonds Lock Desk can upload it to the file

Non-Agency/Non-QM Products Helpful Tips

Special procedures for Jumbo, Non-Agency, and Non-QM products

Non-Agency Lock Desk Phone

For urgent follow-up on Non-Agency/Non-QM requests, call: 925-983-3230

I can't get pricing to populate when trying to do a Non-QM loan. Any tips?
Our training team has put together informational guidance on what to enter in the system for the specific doc type for Non-QM product selections. If you are still having issues or getting disqualifiers in Optimal Blue, please reach out to NonAgencyLocks@cmgfi.com with a screenshot of the error.
What is the cutoff for locking a Jumbo/Non Agency/Non-QM loan in the system?
4:00pm PT (for most of our Jumbo and Non Agency products)
I just requested a lock for a Non QM CMG product. Why isn't it reflecting locked in Byte/Clear yet?
The Non Agency Lock Desk has to review these requests as they come in to assure that all the necessary fields in the system are correctly selected. Due to the complexity of these products and the doc type options, DSCR (if applicable), DTI, etc., we want to ensure the data that we have is accurate in all systems (Byte/OB/Clear).
I cannot get pricing, and I keep getting disqualifiers, what can I do?
You will need to find the product that you are trying to lock with and hit the show button to the far right of that disqualifier to see what is preventing you from getting pricing. You will also need to clear that disqualifier by correcting the information that is causing that hard stop.
I cannot price this loan because the Fico, Loan amount or LTV does not fit within guidelines. How do I get pricing?
Is an exception being requested with a specific investor via producthelp@cmgfi.com or Underwriter? If so, the lock desk can send you pricing manually once confirmed. Please provide evidence of written exception approval from Product help or the underwriter.
How do I price a DSCR Foreign National with no Fico and no Social Security #?
You would use a 680 Fico to price out the loan and the Foreign National will need to be chosen in the dropdown.
Are float downs allowed on Non-Agency loans?
Chase is the only investor currently who allows a floatdown, and it will need to be requested with them first before we can allow.
Can you lock at the current market rate after a lock has been expired for 30 days?
No. Non-Agency locks can be locked current market after being expired after 60 days.
Can my loan be locked for 10 days?
The 10-day lock option is not allowed for any Non-Agency/Jumbo products.

Important Non-Agency Rules

  • No 10-day lock option for Non-Agency/Jumbo
  • Current market relock only after 60 days expired (not 30)
  • Float downs only with Chase investor (request from them first)
  • Lock cutoff is 4:00pm PT for most products
