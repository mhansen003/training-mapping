Email Subject Heading Format

Include the following in your email subject:

  • Borrower's last name and complete loan # with "J" loan # prefix
  • For FHA Brokered Out, include the WPH loan # as well
  • Nature of the request (Lock Extension, Program Change)
  • Loan program or product type (Conventional, Jumbo, 101, Non-QM, etc.)

Best Practice

The body of the email should be clear and concise, outlining the specific request in detail. If something looks off, provide screenshots and examples.

General FAQs

Frequently asked questions about lock desk operations
Q

I have an urgent request. How do I reach a live person?

+

If you have already submitted your request via email and need to follow up with the lock desk for time sensitive files, give us a call at 925-983-3225.

Note

If your call goes to voicemail, please leave a detailed message including loan number and nature of the request and a lock desk specialist will assist as soon as possible.

Q

How do I extend my lock? How much does it cost?

+

Lock extensions for most loans can be completed via Optimal Blue:

  1. Use the Change Request icon
  2. Select the Lock Extension radio button

Cost: The cost for Agency and Jumbo lock extension is 0.02 per day.

For full instructions, please see the Optimal Blue Pricing Manual.

Q

Does CMG offer overnight price protection?

+

Yes! Please see the Joint Ventures Lock Policy link for full details.

Process:

  1. Notify the lock desk via email prior to 8:30AM EST to hold pricing for the previous day
  2. Lock the loan in the system
  3. Contact the lock desk via email to adjust the pricing to the previous day
Q

How do I change loan parameters on my locked loan?

+

Changes to locked loans can be processed through Optimal Blue's Profile Change Request feature:

  1. Select the Change Request icon
  2. Choose the 'Profile Change' radio button
  3. Make the necessary changes to your loan amount, appraised value, etc.
  4. Select the lock icon and proceed with the changes

For full instructions, please see the Optimal Blue Pricing Manual.

Q

I need to switch my loan from Fannie to Freddie or Conv to FHA. How do I do it?

+

Use Optimal Blue's Profile Change Request feature:

  1. Choose the Profile Change button
  2. Review the loan parameters
  3. Select the appropriate Loan Type
  4. Hit Submit - you should see the list of eligible products and pricing
  5. Hit the lock icon and proceed with the change

For full instructions, please see the Optimal Blue Pricing Manual.

Q

My lock expired. How do I relock?

+

Relocks can be performed through the system by accessing OB through Byte/Clear for Agency and Jumbo products:

  1. Select the Change Request icon
  2. Select the radio button to Relock

For full instructions, please see the Optimal Blue Pricing Manual.

Q

I need to change my rate or view rate options from when I initially locked. Where do I go?

+
  1. Launch out to OB through Byte/Clear
  2. Go to the Change Request icon
  3. Select Profile Change and hit submit
  4. The rate stack from the original day will appear
  5. If you wish to change the rate, select the new rate desired and hit the lock icon to apply changes
  6. If not, just close out of the page when done
Q

Rates have improved since I locked. Can I float down?

+

Yes! Joint Ventures offers a one-time float down option on Agency loans.

Please refer to the LOCK POLICY link (page #4) for additional information.

Process:

  1. Access OB through Byte/Clear
  2. Select the Change Request icon
  3. Select the radio button to Float Down
Q

What is the difference between a General Lender Credit and a Net Lender Credit?

+
1. Net Lender Credit

A price improvement adjustment that is typically used to lower the discount points being charged to the borrower. A net lender credit resulting in an overage (price above par) can also be used toward the borrower's closing costs.

2. General Lender Credit

A lump sum lender credit that is itemized on the LE and CD. Important: Once disclosed to the borrower, a general lender credit cannot be reduced or removed.

Q

I cannot find this product in Optimal Blue. How do I price it?

+

Some products are handled manually as they are not automated in the system. Please reach out to the appropriate lock desk inbox for assistance with the following programs:

  • AIO's - email jvcapmkts@cmgfi.com
  • Bond/HFA products - email jvcapmkts@cmgfi.com
  • Chenoa - email jvcapmkts@cmgfi.com
  • CMG 2nds - email jvcapmkts@cmgfi.com
  • Near Miss and Alt A - email jvcapmkts@cmgfi.com
Q

I would like to lock my AIO loan. How do I lock it?

+

Important Requirement

AIO products must be in UW Approved status prior to locking.

Once it's approved, email jvcapmkts@cmgfi.com with:

  • Loan number
  • Last name of borrower
  • Desired margin

The lock desk will lock it in for you.

Bonds and HFA Products Helpful Tips

Bond lock procedures and common questions
Q

I have a bond lock. How do I get it locked in the system?

+

If you have the commitment completed from the HFA site:

  1. Email jvcapmkts@cmgfi.com
  2. Attach the commitment confirmation
  3. Include the loan #

The desk will lock the loan in the LOS system.

See The Bond/DPA Loan workflow and input guide for full detail.

Q

I need to extend my bond lock.

+

Lock desk (jvcapmkts@cmgfi.com) requires an updated lock commitment from the HFA showing that the lock has been extended.

Acceptable documentation:

  • Lock commitment with new expiration date
  • Email from bond agency
  • Extension form approved by the agency
Q

Why do I not get the same lock expiration as the one on my commitment?

+

CMG adds time for delivery and settlement to each lock.

Items That Tend to Delay the Locking Process on Bonds

  • When locking with a 1st, 2nd and 3rd loan, make sure the 2nd and 3rd are created in Byte and already synchronized
  • When making reservations on the HFA site, confirm it is for the total loan amount, not the base loan amount (account for the MIP). Provide base and total loan amount to prevent back and forth
  • Accuracy on property information: street address, city, and zip code
  • When requesting lock, attach a PDF of the actual commitment confirmation from the HFA site - not just a screenshot. There should be an actual confirmation so the Bonds Lock Desk can upload it to the file

Non-Agency/Non-QM Products Helpful Tips

Special considerations for Jumbo and Non-QM products
Q

I sent an email and need to follow up as this is urgent. Who do I call?

+
  • JV Lock Desk: 925-983-3225
  • NonAgency Lock Desk: 925-983-3230
Q

I can't get pricing to populate when trying to do a Non-QM loan. Any tips?

+

Our training team has put together informational guidance on what to enter in the system for the specific doc type for our Non QM product selections.

If you are still having issues or getting disqualifiers in Optimal Blue, please reach out to:

  • JV Lock Desk
  • NonAgency Lock Desk: NonAgencyLocks@cmgfi.com

Include a screenshot of the error and our team can assist.

Q

What is the cutoff for locking a Jumbo/Non Agency/Non-QM loan in the system?

+

4:00pm PT (for most of our Jumbo and Non Agency products)

Q

I just requested a lock for a Non QM CMG product. Why isn't it reflecting locked in Byte/Clear yet?

+

The Non Agency Lock Desk has to review these requests as they come in to assure that all the necessary fields in the system are correctly selected.

Due to the complexity of these products and the doc type options, DSCR (if applicable), DTI, etc., we want to ensure the data that we have is accurate in all systems (Byte/OB/Clear).

Q

I cannot get pricing, and I keep getting disqualifiers, what can I do?

+
  1. Find the product that you are trying to lock
  2. Hit the show button to the far right of that disqualifier to see what is preventing you from getting pricing
  3. Clear that disqualifier by correcting the information that is causing that hard stop
Q

I cannot price this loan because the FICO, Loan amount or LTV does not fit within guidelines. How do I get pricing?

+

Is an exception being requested with a specific investor via the producthelp@cmgfi.com team or Underwriter?

If so, the lock desk can send you pricing manually once confirmed.

Requirement

Please provide evidence of written exception approval from Product help or the underwriter.

Q

How do I price a DSCR Foreign National with no FICO and no Social Security #?

+

You would use a 680 FICO to price out the loan and the Foreign National will need to be chosen in the dropdown.

Q

Are float downs allowed on Non-Agency loans?

+

Chase is the only investor currently who allows a floatdown, and it will need to be requested with them first before we can allow.

Q

Can you lock at the current market rate after a lock has been expired for 30 days?

+

No. Non-Agency locks can be locked at current market after being expired for 60 days.

Q

Can my loan be locked for 10 days?

+

The 10-day lock option is not allowed for any Non-Agency/Jumbo products.
